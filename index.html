Our Story

Born from the
European Gentleman's Code.

Euro Polo was founded on a simple belief: the modern Asian gentleman deserves accessories that reflect a European standard of craft — without compromise.

We curate and design each collection with a clean European aesthetic — minimal, structural, purposeful. From the boardroom to the airport lounge, from a weekend in Kuala Lumpur to a business trip in London, Euro Polo accompanies every chapter.

Our core range — bags, wallets and belts — is complemented by our Luggage Extension Line, designed for the man who travels in style.

Minimal No excess. Clean, considered design.
Business Ready Professional enough for any room.
Sport Gentleman Active lifestyle, refined execution.
